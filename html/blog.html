Emotion, Stress, and Health

The video Emotion, Stress, and Health was about how emotions affect health. The three main reasons of how emotions can affect health is because of lifestyle and behavioral factors, social factors, and biological factors. Facial feedback hypothesis is when smiling can make your mood happier, even when not happy. Being depressed can make you unhealthy. Stress can also be bad for your health and could be a cause of heart disease which is very common in the U.S. Stress can increase blood pressure, heart rate, and cholesterol which plays a huge factor to getting heart disease. The viewers should keep in mind the way they handle their emotions such as being depressed, angry, or stressed because it could lead to a negative health outcome. Emotions can be changed which means everyone could try to be happier and healthier. I liked this video because it was informative, and I have learned to always have a positive mindset even when down. The video gives motivation to be happier because it could affect out health in a good way.
